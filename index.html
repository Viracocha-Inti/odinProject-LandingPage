The Most Terrifying of Them All

The black dragon is the biggest of all dragons. Careful, its fire may not be the strongest among the dragon family, but its physical prowess is definitely one to watch out for.

The red dragons are verage-sized dragons, but their fire breath is among the strongest in their species. No matter how far away you are, you may not be far away to be safe from its breath.

The blue dragons are large dragons that also have the ability to breath electricity onto its foes. Be specially careful with these dragons if you are anywhere near water. The are specially dangerous during rainy weather.

The green dragons are small-sized dragons, that can fly extremely fast. These dragons are able to spit out acid and noxious gases from their mouth. There have been recordings of their acid-breath melting away castle walls.
